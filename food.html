3 Classes of Food

  1. Carbohydrates
  2. Proteins
  3. Fats

Carbohydrates

Provide energy for the body.

Carbohydrates
Carbohydrates provide the body's primary source of energy, especially for the brain and muscles during activity. They are found in foods like grains, fruits, and vegetables.

5 examples

  1. Bread
  2. Rice
  3. Pasta
  4. Potatoes
  5. Yam

Proteins

Help in body growth and repair.

Proteins
Proteins are essential for building and repairing tissues, supporting immune function, and are found in foods like meat, fish, eggs, and legumes.

5 examples

  1. Beans
  2. Eggs
  3. Fish
  4. Meat

Fats

Fats: Provide energy and support cell growth

Fats
Fats supply long-lasting energy, support cell function, and help absorb vitamins, with sources including oils, butter, and avocados.
  1. Cheese
  2. Nuts
  3. avocados
  4. Butter
  5. Oils
